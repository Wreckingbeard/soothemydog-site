Why Soothe My Dog

Calm, steady music for the everyday moments that leave a dog uneasy.

Dogs are creatures of comfort and routine. When the house goes quiet, when the sky starts to rumble, or when a puppy faces a first night in a new home, a little steadiness goes a long way. Soothe My Dog exists to give your dog that steadiness: hours of gentle, unbroken music you can leave playing whenever your dog needs to settle.

Who it is for

  • Dogs who are home alone while their people are at work or out for the day.
  • Dogs frightened by fireworks and thunderstorms who need a calm sound to focus on.
  • Dogs at bedtime who settle more easily with soft, continuous music.
  • New puppies during first nights, crate training, and daytime naps.
  • Senior dogs who rest better with gentle company in the room.

How our music is made

Every Soothe My Dog video has its own music, composed for that one video. Gentle piano, warm strings, a little soft ambience, in a calm key at a slow, resting tempo. We keep it free of loops and abrupt changes, because a sudden shift can startle a dog awake. Every finished video gets a review before it goes out, then we pair it with calming imagery that is easy on the eyes.

We also keep the music fresh: rather than replaying the same track, we create new, varied sessions. Research on dogs suggests that variety helps calming music keep its effect over time.

A note from us: Calming music can help many dogs relax, but it is not a substitute for veterinary care. If your dog shows signs of severe anxiety, distress, or a medical problem, please talk with your veterinarian about a plan that fits your dog.
