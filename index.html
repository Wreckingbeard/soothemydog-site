Original music, made for anxious dogs

Every Soothe My Dog video features an original composition created just for that video - gentle piano, warm pads, and soft strings at a calm, steady tempo, paired with sleepy-puppy imagery. No loops, no jarring changes, nothing to startle a resting dog.

How to use it

Press play when you leave for work, at bedtime, or before the fireworks start. Our long-form videos run for hours so your dog can settle without interruption. If your dog has severe anxiety or a medical condition, please talk to your veterinarian - music helps, but it is not a treatment.
