About Soothe My Dog

Made by someone who spent a career keeping people calm under pressure, now pointing that same instinct at dogs.

Soothe My Dog is a small, independent project. It was started by Ray, a retired U.S. Army combat medic who spent sixteen years learning how much a calm environment matters when nerves are frayed. After service, that same instinct turned toward the family dog, and a plain realization: a lot of dogs spend a lot of hours anxious and alone.

So we make what we wished existed: hours of gentle, original music, made to help a dog settle, with no loops, no jarring changes, and calming imagery to match. Every video is put together and reviewed by hand before it goes out, because your dog deserves the good stuff.

If our music helps your dog rest a little easier, that is the whole point.
