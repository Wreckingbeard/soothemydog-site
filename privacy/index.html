Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026 · Soothe My Dog (soothemydog.com)

The short version

This website is a static informational site. It does not collect, store, or share personal information, does not set cookies, and does not run analytics or advertising trackers.

Information we collect

None. You can browse this site without creating an account or submitting any personal data. If you email us at wreckingbeard@gmail.com, we receive what you send and use it only to reply.

Our software ("Soothe My Dog Publisher") and YouTube API Services

Soothe My Dog operates an internal publishing tool that uses YouTube API Services to upload and manage videos on our own YouTube channel. This tool:

  • is used only by the channel owner and is not available to the public;
  • accesses only our own channel's data via Google OAuth, with the owner's consent;
  • does not access, collect, store, or share any third-party user data or any YouTube user data belonging to anyone other than the owner;
  • stores its own authorization tokens securely on the owner's private servers and nowhere else.

Because the tool uses YouTube API Services, users of YouTube are also subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy. Any Google-account authorization granted to our tool can be revoked at any time via Google security settings.

Affiliate links

Some pages or video descriptions may contain Amazon affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. Amazon may use cookies on its own site per its own policies; we do not receive personal information from those purchases beyond aggregate commission reporting.

Children's privacy

This site and our content are directed at adult pet owners, not children, and we knowingly collect no information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If our practices change, this page will be updated with a new effective date.
