Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026 · soothemydog.com

The short version

This website is a static informational site. It does not collect, store, or share personal information, does not set cookies, and does not run analytics or advertising trackers.

Information we collect

None. You can browse this site without an account or submitting any personal data. If you email us, we receive what you send and use it only to reply.

Our publishing software and YouTube API Services

Soothe My Dog operates a private, internal publishing tool that uses YouTube API Services to upload and manage videos on our own YouTube channel. This tool:

  • is used only by the channel owner and is not available to the public;
  • accesses only our own channel's data via Google OAuth, with the owner's consent;
  • does not access, collect, store, or share any third-party user data or any YouTube user data belonging to anyone other than the owner;
  • stores its own authorization tokens securely on the owner's private servers and nowhere else.

Because the tool uses YouTube API Services, use of YouTube is also governed by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy. Any Google-account authorization granted to our tool can be revoked at any time via Google security settings.

Affiliate links

Some pages or video descriptions may contain Amazon affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. We do not receive personal information from those purchases beyond aggregate commission reporting.

Children's privacy

This site and our content are directed at adult pet owners, not children, and we knowingly collect no information from anyone.

Changes & contact

If our practices change, this page will be updated with a new date. Contact: wreckingbeard@gmail.com.
